# Banking Sector

## Overview
Largest sector in Nifty. Split into PSU banks and private banks.

## Sub-sectors
- **Private Banks:** HDFC, ICICI, Kotak, Axis
- **PSU Banks:** SBI, PNB, Bank of Baroda
- **Small Finance Banks:** AU Bank, Equitas
- **NBFCs:** Bajaj Finance, Shriram Finance

## Key Metrics to Watch
| Metric | Good | Bad |
|--------|------|-----|
| NIM (Net Interest Margin) | >3.5% | <3% |
| GNPA (Gross NPA) | <2% | >5% |
| CASA Ratio | >40% | <30% |
| ROA | >1.5% | <1% |
| Credit Growth | >15% | <10% |

## Drivers
- RBI interest rate policy
- Credit growth cycle
- Asset quality (NPAs)
- Digital banking adoption

## Headwinds  
- Rising NPAs in economic downturns
- Fintech competition
- Interest rate compression

## Seasonality
- Strong: Oct-Mar (credit demand)
- Watch: Budget (Feb) for PSU bank announcements

## Private vs PSU
| Aspect | Private | PSU |
|--------|---------|-----|
| Efficiency | Higher | Lower |
| NPA | Lower | Higher |
| Valuation | Premium | Discount |
| Growth | Faster | Slower |

## Trading Notes
- Bank Nifty is highly liquid for F&O
- Results heavily impact stock moves
- Watch RBI policy dates
