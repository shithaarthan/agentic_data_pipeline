# Information Technology Sector

## Overview
Indian IT sector is export-driven with major revenue from US/Europe.

## Key Characteristics
- USD revenue (INR depreciation = positive)
- High margins (20-25% operating margin)
- Cash-rich, low debt companies
- Regular dividends and buybacks

## Major Players
| Company | Focus Area |
|---------|-----------|
| TCS | BFSI, enterprise |
| Infosys | Digital transformation |
| Wipro | Cloud, cybersecurity |
| HCL Tech | Infrastructure, products |
| Tech Mahindra | Telecom, 5G |
| LTIMindtree | Engineering services |

## Drivers
- US tech spending
- Digital transformation demand
- Cloud migration deals
- AI/ML projects

## Headwinds
- US recession fears
- AI replacing jobs narrative
- H1B visa restrictions
- Wage inflation

## Seasonality
- Strong: Oct-Mar (Q3-Q4 results)
- Weak: Apr-Jun (visa/budget season uncertainty)

## Valuation Benchmarks
- P/E range: 20-35x for large caps
- P/E < 25: Attractive
- P/E > 35: Expensive

## Correlation
- Inversely correlated with INR strength
- Follows US tech sentiment (NASDAQ)
